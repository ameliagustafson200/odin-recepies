Lasagna

Lasagna is a tasty italian dish that has been around since the middle ages. It typically includes cheeses such as parmesan, mozzerella, etc., meat and sauce. The origin of the word lasagna dates back a while, in ancient rome there was a similar dish referred to as lasana or lasanum. But there are also other theories as to where we got the word lasagna today.

Ingredients:
  • Ground beef
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg
Steps:
  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
    • Mix the ricotta and egg
  4. Layer the lasagna in this order:
    1. Meat sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta mixture
    4. Mozzarella slices
    5. Meat sauce
    6. Parmesan cheese
    7. Repeat
  5. Cover with aluminum foil and bake(50 mins @ 375 degrees)
  6. Let cool before serving
