Air Fryer Sweet Potatoes

Making Sweet potatoes is a simle and healthy option to make.

Ingredients:
  • Sweet Potatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic Pepper(and any other seasonings you may want to use)
  • Salt and pepper
Steps:
  1. Wash and peel(optional) sweet potatoes
  2. Cut into half to one inch cubes
  3. Toss them with the seasonings and olive oil
  4. Lay evenly in air fryer for 10-12 minutes (shaking the basket at 6 minutes) @ 400 degrees
