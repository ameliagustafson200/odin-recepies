Chocolate Chip Cookies

Chocolate chip cookies are a classic sweet

Ingredients:
  • Cookie Dough(store bought or hand made), if you are hand-making it here is what you'll need for that
    • Butter
    • Eggs
    • Vanilla
    • Baking Soda
    • Water
    • Salt
    • Flour
    • Chocolate chips
Steps:
  1. If you are hand-making the dough, this is how you should mix the ingredients:
    1. Beat the butter and sugar, then beat in the eggs and vanilla
    2. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture.
    3. Stir in the flour, chocolate chips, any other topping you would like in the cookies (i.e nuts)
    4. Put onto a baking sheet
    5. Cook until the edges are golden brown
  2. If you are cooking store bought cookie dough, follow the instructions on the package, other wise just put into the oven until the edges are golden brown.
